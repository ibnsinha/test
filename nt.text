Upcoming Change: EC2 Public AMI and EBS Snapshot Sharing Restrictions

What is happening?

Between Nov 6 – Nov 10, the Cloud Environment Engineering team will implement changes to restrict public sharing of EC2 resources across our AWS accounts.

The following controls will be enforced:
	•	Amazon Machine Images (AMIs): Public sharing will be restricted
	•	EBS Snapshots: Public sharing will be restricted

⸻

What is the impact?
	•	New AMIs and EBS snapshots cannot be shared publicly
	•	Any existing public sharing configurations may be restricted or require review
	•	Workloads that rely on publicly shared AMIs or snapshots may be impacted

⸻

What action is required?
	•	Please review your workloads and ensure they do not depend on publicly shared AMIs or EBS snapshots
	•	If public sharing is required for a valid business case, please contact the Cloud Environment Engineering team in advance
	•	Ensure appropriate access controls (IAM roles, private sharing) are used instead
